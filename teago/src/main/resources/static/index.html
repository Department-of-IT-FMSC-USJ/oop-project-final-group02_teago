What TeaGo Offers

Everything in One Platform

From daily deliveries to monthly financial statements — TeaGo handles every step of your tea factory's operations.

🍃

Tea Delivery Tracking

Record daily tea leaf deliveries with land owner details and weight. Instant dashboard updates for full visibility.

💰

Automated Payments

Payments are automatically calculated based on tea weight and configured rate per kg — zero manual errors.

🌿

Fertilizer Management

Land owners request fertilizers; factory owners approve and auto-deduct costs from monthly billing.

📋

Loan & Advance System

Structured loan application, approval workflow, and automatic cheque generation with supervisor delivery.

📊

Monthly Bills & Reports

Generate detailed monthly bills and income statements (3 or 6 months) for transparency and record-keeping.

👷

Employee Management

Manage worker records, track work days, and auto-calculate salaries — all within the platform.

🔒

Secure Authentication

Role-based access control ensures that land owners, factory owners, and supervisors see only what they need.

📣

Complaint Management

Land owners can submit complaints directly to the factory. Factories respond within the system seamlessly.

User Roles

Built for Every Stakeholder

TeaGo supports three distinct roles — each with a tailored dashboard and feature set.

🧑‍🌾

Tea Land Owner

Track your deliveries, manage your workers, and stay informed about your monthly earnings.

  • Record daily tea deliveries
  • Request fertilizers & loans
  • View monthly bills
  • Manage employee salaries
  • Submit complaints
  • Generate income statements
🏭

Factory Owner

Oversee all operations, approve requests, issue cheques, and view comprehensive reports.

  • Approve loans & fertilizer
  • Generate monthly bills
  • Issue advance cheques
  • View all land owner data
  • Respond to complaints
  • Approve income statements
🚚

Supervisor

Collect tea leaves from land owners and deliver cheques — with full status tracking.

  • View assigned deliveries
  • Deliver advance cheques
  • Update delivery status
  • Verify cheque receipt
